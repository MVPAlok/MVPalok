- 🌱 **Currently Learning/Exploring:** Advanced interactive 3D experiences in Spline and expanding my Next.js architecture skills.
- 💼 **Previous Experience:** UI/UX Intern at **Sync and Explore** & Graphic Design Intern at **Mentorsity**.
- 🏆 **Achievements:** Earned the **Spline Expert Badge** on Contra & selected for the **Smart India Hackathon 2025**.
